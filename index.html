Honey the Dog

A Miniature Husky

The years she has spent with us:

  • 2005 - Became 1 of our family at the age of 1
  • 2009 - She grew bigger but not the regular size of any husky, then she was transferred to Sagada cause she can't roam properly in the city. And we had to say goodbye.
  • 2010 - She then get moved to Bontoc and under the care of our cousin
  • 2012 - Our family then moved to Itogon at this year, and finally we got to see our Honey again. And we were so happy to see her again
  • 2013 - At this year, she then gave birth to 2 cross-breed puppies and looks exactly like her, but sadly just few months past the puppies did not make it.
  • 2014 - We took Honey out with us during vacation, we went to a beach played with her until night time and had fun all the way.
  • 2015 - Well, congrats again...she gave birth to 2 puppies again, this time just few months our neighbours adopted the puppies and cared them like family.
  • 2016 - Sadly, we noticed she wasn't how she exactly the past years we've been together. We went to a vet to have her checked up, they said that she is almost at that time of her age.
  • 2017 - At the beginning of the year she passed away, We will never forgot her because she made us happy even though she was away for 3 years, she still remembered us.
